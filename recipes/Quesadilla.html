Quesadilla

A simple yet very delicious meal that can be very unique due to the fact that anything can be added to it. while chicken or beef can be added, we will keep it very simple. Let's do this!

Ingredients

  • Corn or flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheese of your choice
  • Spatula (if needed to flip the quesadilla)

Steps

  1. First bring your griddle to medium heat and begind to lay your tortilla on the griddle.
  2. Use your Spatula (or your own hand) and flip the tortilla
  3. Finally add your cheese and fold the tortilla over the cheese and flip the tortilla one more time until cheese is melted.
  4. Enjoy!
