Mashed Potatoes

Ah yes! Nothing like a huge pot of warm and buttery mashed potatoes. Mashed potatoes is a classic dish and perfect for any family and dinner occasion.

Here is what you will need to get started:

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of peeled Potatoes
  • 2 tablespoon of butter butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. First, bring potatoes to a boil in a pot.
  2. Drain out excess water and place potatoes in a large bowl and begin to smash the potatoes.
  3. In a sauce pan, warm butter and milk and begin to add the sauce into mashed potatoes and begin to mix the two until smooth and buttery.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  5. Enjoy!
