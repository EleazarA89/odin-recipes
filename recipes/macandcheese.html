Macaroni and Cheese

Macaroni is the best dish for any occasion! It's a delicious meal and very quick to make! Here is what you need and how to make it.

Ingredients

  • Packaged elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Milk
  • All purpose flour

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to the package directions and drain the excess water.
  2. Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan.
  3. Slowly begin to stir in the all purpose flour until sauce gets smooth
  4. Add the milk and stir constantly.
  5. Add cheese and bring the heat down to low and continue to stir until the sauce is thick.
  6. Grab cooked pasta and put it in a large casserole dish.
  7. Slowly begin to add the sauce on top of the macaroni and place the dish in the oven at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
